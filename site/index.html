What is WARBIRD?

WARBIRD is a community-driven memecoin on the Solana blockchain. With a fixed supply of 100 million tokens, zero buy/sell tax, and permanently revoked mint authority, WARBIRD represents the next generation of transparent, community-owned tokens.

While the world watches the skies, WARBIRD takes flight. No VC, no presale, no insider allocation. Just pure community.
